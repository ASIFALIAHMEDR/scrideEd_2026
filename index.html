The Problem Nobody Talks About

Most Indian Students Don't Touch Real Tech Until They're 20.

By then, they've already chosen a career. Often the wrong one. The window to explore is 13–18 — and almost nobody is serving it properly.

The Knowledge Gap

School science teaches theory. Nobody builds anything. A student who loves "how things work" never discovers they could be an engineer — because they never get to try.

The Wrong Career Choice

Thousands of students pick commerce or arts because nobody showed them that tech can be creative, hands-on, and exciting. The window to explore closes by Class 12.

The Late Start

The world's best engineers started building at 14. Most Indian students hold a microcontroller for the first time at 20. The gap isn't talent — it's access and timing.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Does my child need any prior experience?
None at all. Our workshops are built from the ground up for complete beginners. If your teen is curious, that's the only requirement.
What age group is ScrideEd for?
We focus specifically on students aged 13–18 — from middle school through Class 12 / PUC. This is the window we believe matters most for career exploration.
Where are the workshops held?
Our base is at #39, B-Type Quarters, Robertson Road, Frazer Town, Bangalore. We also conduct workshops at partner schools and community locations across the city.
What will my child actually build?
Depending on the course: a fully functional programmable robot, a working IoT device using Arduino, or real Python programs. Every course ends with a tangible project your child can demo and be proud of.
What is your refund policy?
Full refund if you cancel more than 48 hours before the workshop. 50% refund between 24–48 hours. No refund within 24 hours, but we'll offer priority registration for the next batch.
Is ScrideEd a registered company?
Yes. ScrideEd is a brand under Scride Smart Solutions Private Limited, a registered company based in Bangalore, Karnataka, India.
