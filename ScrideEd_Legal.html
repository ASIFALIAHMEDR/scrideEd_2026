Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal information ScrideEd collects, how we use it, and your rights. We keep it simple because we believe you deserve to understand it.

01

Who We Are

ScrideEd is a brand operated by Scride Smart Solutions Private Limited, registered in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. Our website is scrideed.com. For any privacy-related matter, contact us at hello@scrideed.com or +91 8660552494.

02

What We Collect

We only collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Student details — name, age, class, school name
  • Parent / guardian details — name, phone number, email, area
  • Workshop registration and payment information
  • Messages and enquiries submitted via our website or WhatsApp
  • Basic analytics data via Google Analytics (page visits, device type — no personal identifiers)
03

How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm workshop registrations and send course-related communication
  • To process payments and issue receipts
  • To respond to your enquiries via WhatsApp or email
  • To send updates about new workshops and courses (you can opt out at any time)
  • To improve our services using anonymised usage data
We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to any third party — ever.
04

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data only with:

  • Payment processors (UPI / Razorpay) to complete transactions
  • Communication tools (WhatsApp Business, Google Workspace) necessary to operate our services
  • Legal authorities if required by applicable Indian law
05

Photos & Videos

We only photograph or video students during workshops when explicit written permission has been granted by a parent or guardian via our registration form. If you did not grant permission, your child will not be photographed. You may withdraw permission at any time by emailing hello@scrideed.com.

06

Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose it was collected for, or as required by Indian law. Registration records are kept for a minimum of 3 years for accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements).

07

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (where legally permissible)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@scrideed.com. We will respond within 30 days.

08

Cookies

Our website uses basic cookies for analytics (Google Analytics) and functionality. These do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use the site.

09

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, contact us immediately at hello@scrideed.com.

Privacy questions?Email us or WhatsApp us — we read every message personally.

Refund Policy

We want every family to feel safe registering with us. Our refund policy is straightforward — no confusing clauses, no chasing us for what you're owed.

01

Workshop & Course Registrations

When you cancel Refund
More than 48 hours before the workshop 100% refund
Between 24 and 48 hours before the workshop 50% refund
Less than 24 hours before, or no-show No refund*
*If you miss a workshop due to a genuine emergency, contact us. We will offer priority registration for the next available batch at no additional cost — we're human about it.
02

Kit Purchases

  • Kits may be returned within 7 days of delivery if unused and in original, undamaged packaging
  • Opened or used kits are not eligible for return
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer
  • Refunds for eligible kit returns are processed within 7 working days
03

If ScrideEd Cancels or Reschedules

If we cancel or significantly reschedule a workshop for any reason, you will receive:

  • A 100% refund, or
  • Priority registration for the next available batch — your choice

We will notify you via WhatsApp as early as possible if this situation arises.

04

School Engagements

Refund terms for school lab setup, curriculum, and teacher training engagements are governed by the specific agreement signed with the school prior to engagement. Please refer to your school's service agreement for details.

05

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us before your workshop date:

  • WhatsApp: +91 8660552494
  • Email: hello@scrideed.com

Include your full name, registered phone number, and workshop name. We process eligible refunds within 5–7 working days to the original payment method.
