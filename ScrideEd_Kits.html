Kit FAQs

Do I need any experience to use these kits? +
The Beginner Arduino Kit and Electronics Lab Kit are designed for complete beginners. The Robotics Explorer Kit assumes basic familiarity with Arduino. The Raspberry Pi Kit suits students who already know some Python or Linux basics.
What's included in "project guide"? +
Every kit includes a printed or digital step-by-step project guide written by Asif — covering setup, component explanation, and multiple project walkthroughs. You also get WhatsApp access to ask questions directly.
Do you deliver outside Bangalore? +
Currently we focus on Bangalore delivery. If you're outside Bangalore, WhatsApp us and we'll check if we can arrange shipping to your location.
How long does delivery take? +
We dispatch within 1–2 working days of payment confirmation. Delivery within Bangalore typically takes 1–3 days. We'll send you tracking details on WhatsApp.
What if a component in the kit is missing or damaged? +
WhatsApp us immediately with a photo and we'll replace the component at no charge. We check every kit before dispatch but occasionally components get damaged in transit.
Is it cheaper to buy the kit separately or with a workshop? +
Workshop registrations include the kit at no extra cost — making the bundle significantly better value. If you only want the kit without the workshop, you can order it separately at the listed price.
