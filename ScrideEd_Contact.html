Common Questions

Quick Answers

How quickly do you respond?

WhatsApp is the fastest — typically within a few hours on Mon–Sat. Email replies within 24 hours. We don't use bots; every message is read by a real person.

Can I visit your location?

Yes — we're at #39, B-Type Quarters, Robertson Road, Frazer Town. Please WhatsApp us first to confirm we're available before visiting.

How do I register for a workshop?

Visit our registration page and fill the form. We'll WhatsApp you within a few hours to confirm your seat and share payment details.

I'm a school — who should I contact?

Email us at hello@scrideed.com with your school name and what you're looking for. We respond to school enquiries within 24 hours.

Do you offer online workshops?

Our current focus is in-person, hands-on workshops in Bangalore. The physical experience is central to what we do. Online options may be explored in future.

Is ScrideEd a registered company?

Yes. ScrideEd is a brand under Scride Smart Solutions Private Limited, a registered company in Bangalore, Karnataka, India.
